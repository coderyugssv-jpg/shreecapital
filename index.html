Our historical accuracy is approximately 75-80% on call targets. Koi bhi 100% guarantee nahi de sakta—market mein yeh possible hi nahi. Jo honestly nahi bolte, unse dur raho. We show real results, no cherry-picking.
Trading ke liye kitna capital chahiye? +
Minimum ₹50,000–₹1 Lakh recommended for options trading. Kam capital mein risk management mushkil ho jaata hai. Options mein ek lot = generally 50–75 shares. Apni risk capacity ke hisaab se plan karo. Hum guidance denge.

Market open hone se pehle (9:00–9:15 AM) pre-market analysis milta hai. Intraday calls 9:15 AM ke baad market movement dekh ke bhejte hain. Usually 2-3 calls per day—quality over quantity hamare liye important hai.
Naya trader hun, kya mujhe samajh aayega? +
Bilkul! Har call mein hum explain karte hain kyun yeh call le rahe hain—plain language mein. Entry, Target, Stoploss clearly define hota hai. Confusion ho toh 1-on-1 support bhi available hai. Aap akele nahi ho.
